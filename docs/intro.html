Molecular Quantum Mechanics#

Welcome to the course in Molecular Quantum Mechanics (CB2070)!

In this course, we will study wave functions for many-electron systems, or more specifically molecules. To our help we will have a modern Python-driven quantum chemistry software named VeloxChem [RLV+20] that enables an exploration of the underlying data structures and easy script-based interactions.

Such an interactive approach to learn about quantum chemistry is the topic of the eChem book [FDB+22].
